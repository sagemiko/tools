Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 27, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Sage Miko ("the App", "we", "us") handles information in connection with your use of the App. We are committed to keeping data collection to a minimum and to being transparent about how the App works.

1. Information We Collect

The App collects only the following limited data, and only as needed to operate:

  • Purchase information — records of in-app subscription purchases, used to manage your subscription status and unlock paid features.
  • Device identifier — a device-level identifier used by our payment-management provider to associate your purchases with your device and restore them when needed.

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, location, contacts, photos, or browsing history.

2. Birth Data and Astrological Calculations

To generate your readings, the App may ask you to enter birth information (such as a date of birth). This information is processed entirely on your device by an offline calculation engine. It is not transmitted to us or to any third party, and it is not stored on our servers.

3. How We Use Information

The limited data described above is used solely for app functionality — specifically, to process subscriptions and provide the features you have purchased. We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or marketing.

4. Tracking

We do not track you. We do not link your data with data from other companies' apps or websites for advertising or measurement purposes, and we do not share your data with data brokers.

5. Third-Party Services

The App relies on the following services strictly to operate in-app purchases:

  • Apple App Store / In-App Purchase — handles all payment processing. Your payment details are entered with Apple and are never accessible to us.
  • RevenueCat — manages subscription status and purchase validation on our behalf. RevenueCat processes purchase and device-identifier data as a service provider. See RevenueCat's privacy policy at https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

Purchase and device-identifier data is retained only for as long as needed to manage your subscription. You may request information about, or deletion of, data associated with your purchases by contacting us at the address below.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided data to us, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
